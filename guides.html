🗡️ Counter assassins without panic

Top

Most players die to assassins before the fight even starts. Ling, Hayabusa, Gusion, Fanny-type heroes love one thing: a lonely backline hero standing with no vision.

The real counter is not only the hero pick. It is your position. If you are marksman or mage, do not walk into river bushes first. Let tank/roam check, or use a safe skill from distance.

In-game habits

  • Save Flicker/dash/knockback until the assassin commits.
  • Do not clear side lane alone after mid game unless you see enemy jungle.
  • If enemy assassin disappears, play closer to tower or teammate.
  • One defensive item is better than dying with full damage build.

💰 Gold lane survival plan

Top

Gold lane is not about proving you are stronger at level 1. It is about reaching item timing without giving free kills. A marksman with farm can carry. A marksman who dies twice early becomes food.

If roam ignores you, do not blame and overpush. Freeze near tower, last-hit safely and check minimap every few seconds. When you finally get core items, fight behind your frontline.

🧠 Draft without trolling

Top

A lot of games are lost before minions spawn. Not because enemy is too good, but because your team picked five heroes that cannot work together.

Before locking, ask: do we have damage? frontline? CC? wave clear? If the answer is no, your favorite hero may not be the best pick for this match.

🧠 Draft Helper🎯 Pick Score
  • If team already has two marksmen, do not add another squishy damage hero.
  • If nobody can start fights, choose tank/fighter/CC mage.
  • If enemy has heavy dive, pick peel or durability.
  • Counter the enemy win condition, not just one hero name.

🛡️ When roam ignores you

Top

Solo queue roam can be amazing or completely lost. You cannot control that. What you can control is how risky you play when you know nobody is watching your lane.

If roam never comes, stop taking 50/50 fights. Clear wave, stay near safe zones, and play for item timing. The goal is to stay useful until the enemy makes a mistake.

  • Do not walk river alone just to check where enemies are.
  • Use pings, but do not start typing fights.
  • Follow the teammate who is actually playing well.
  • If you cannot get help, play wave and turret first.

🧊 Stop losing streaks

Top

A losing streak is not only matchmaking. After 3–4 losses, most players start forcing fights, blaming teammates, changing heroes and taking worse decisions.

The fix is boring but it works: take a short break, pick comfort heroes, reduce deaths and play objectives. If you are tilted, do not pick high-risk heroes that need perfect mechanics.

🏆 Rank Helper📊 Tier List
  • After 3 losses, stop ranked for a while.
  • Use 2–3 comfort heroes only.
  • Count deaths more than kills. Fewer deaths gives comeback chance.
  • When ahead, take Lord/turret instead of chasing one low HP enemy.
