About HeroCore MLBB

HeroCore MLBB is a fan-made Mobile Legends guide/tools site made by Vidath Lalindu from Sri Lanka.

I built it because MLBB players often need quick answers while drafting: who counters this hero, which duo works, is this pick safe, and how should I play the matchup?

The site is not trying to be a boring wiki. The goal is to feel like a quick MLBB helper: open, choose heroes, get a plan, then go back to the game smarter.

What this site includes

  • Hero pages with builds, counters, gameplay tips, clean skill-slot guides, and related heroes.
  • Tools such as matchup analysis, draft helper, pick score, rank helper, compare, and synergy recommendations.
  • Beginner-friendly explanations focused on practical in-game decisions.

Independent fan project

HeroCore MLBB is not an official Mobile Legends: Bang Bang website. This website is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or officially connected to Moonton or Mobile Legends: Bang Bang.

Accuracy note

Mobile Legends changes through patches, buffs, nerfs, and meta shifts. Guides and recommendations are educational/community-style guidance and may need checking against the current in-game patch.
