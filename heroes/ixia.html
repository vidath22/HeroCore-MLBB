Player Tip

Useful in-game note

Ixia gets value only when she can stand still safely. Do not press ultimate first in messy fights. Wait for hooks, knockups or assassin dives to be used, then ult from behind your tank.

Pro Gameplay Guide

Enhanced
Early Game

Farm safely and avoid unnecessary trades before first core item.

Mid Game

Join fights behind frontline and play around item power spikes.

Late Game

Stay protected, hit the closest safe target, and do not face-check bushes.

Combo guide: Skill 2 setup → Skill 1 poke → basic attacks → Ultimate after enemy dash/CC is used.
Positioning: Stay behind tank/frontline. Do not open ultimate first if hook or hard engage is still available.

When to Pick

  • Your team has a strong frontline
  • Enemy lacks hard dive
  • You can safely reach 2-3 core items
  • You want a Marksman who can play Gold Lane because it needs farm and scales with items.

Avoid Picking When

  • Enemy has multiple dive assassins
  • Your team has no frontline
  • You are forced to first-pick into unknown counters

Advanced Tips

  • Play Ixia around the strongest timing of the Marksman role.
  • Watch the minimap before forcing fights.
  • Save escape or control skills for enemy dive.
  • Win fight → take turret, Turtle/Lord, or enemy jungle. Do not chase forever.
  • Use Ixia when the enemy key escape or control skill is already used.
  • Do not fight randomly before objectives; prepare vision and cooldowns first.
  • After winning a fight, take turret, Turtle, Lord, or enemy jungle instead of chasing too far.
