Player Tip

Useful in-game note

Lancelot needs an exit route before entering. Use minions or jungle monsters as dash paths, then leave after burst instead of staying for a basic-attack fight.

Pro Gameplay Guide

Enhanced
Early Game

Secure jungle tempo and avoid forced fights before level/item timing.

Mid Game

Look for isolated targets and punish enemy backline rotations.

Late Game

Wait for vision and cooldowns before diving; one mistake can lose the fight.

Combo guide: Skill 1 path dash → Skill 2 burst → Skill 1 reset → Ultimate execute → dash out.
Positioning: Use minions/jungle monsters as dash paths. Always keep an exit.

When to Pick

  • Enemy backline has low mobility
  • Your team can provide vision or CC setup
  • You can snowball early jungle fights
  • You want a Assassin who can play Jungle because it can snowball faster with rotations and objectives.

Avoid Picking When

  • Enemy has heavy point-and-click control
  • Targets buy defensive items early
  • Your team lacks vision and setup

Advanced Tips

  • Play Lancelot around the strongest timing of the Assassin role.
  • Watch the minimap before forcing fights.
  • Save escape or control skills for enemy dive.
  • Win fight → take turret, Turtle/Lord, or enemy jungle. Do not chase forever.
  • Use Lancelot when the enemy key escape or control skill is already used.
  • Do not fight randomly before objectives; prepare vision and cooldowns first.
  • After winning a fight, take turret, Turtle, Lord, or enemy jungle instead of chasing too far.
