Player Tip

Useful in-game note

Estes is strongest when the team stays close but not stacked into enemy AoE. Keep healing range, but do not let one enemy ult hit everyone.

Pro Gameplay Guide

Template
Early Game

Protect key lane, give vision, and avoid wasting defensive skills.

Mid Game

Play around the strongest teammate and objective timing.

Late Game

Peel, heal, shield, or set up fights depending on team needs.

Combo guide: Setup/protective skill → follow-up utility → save escape/peel for enemy dive.
Positioning: Stay close enough to help carries but not so close that you get picked first.

When to Pick

  • Your team has strong carries to protect
  • Enemy relies on burst that can be denied
  • You need sustain and utility more than engage
  • You want a Support who can play Roam because it helps the team with vision, peel, and setups.

Avoid Picking When

  • Enemy has strong burst before you can save allies
  • Your team needs a hard tank but has none
  • Enemies can isolate you first

Advanced Tips

  • Play Estes around the strongest timing of the Support role.
  • Watch the minimap before forcing fights.
  • Save escape or control skills for enemy dive.
  • Win fight → take turret, Turtle/Lord, or enemy jungle. Do not chase forever.
  • Use Estes when the enemy key escape or control skill is already used.
  • Do not fight randomly before objectives; prepare vision and cooldowns first.
  • After winning a fight, take turret, Turtle, Lord, or enemy jungle instead of chasing too far.
