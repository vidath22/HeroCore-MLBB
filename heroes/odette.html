Player Tip

Useful in-game note

Odette needs setup and patience. Wait until enemy interrupts are used, or pair with Johnson, Atlas or Tigreal before committing ultimate.

Pro Gameplay Guide

Template
Early Game

Clear mid wave quickly and rotate with roam.

Mid Game

Control choke points and punish enemies walking into skill range.

Late Game

Stay behind frontline and use skills after enemies commit.

Combo guide: Control/poke skill → main damage skill → ultimate when enemies are grouped.
Positioning: Mid/backline. Use walls and bushes to hide skill direction.

When to Pick

  • Enemy composition groups up often
  • Your roam can help you rotate
  • Your team needs magic damage or wave clear
  • You want a Mage who can play Mid Lane because it can rotate quickly to help side lanes.

Avoid Picking When

  • Enemy has many anti-burst or magic defense items
  • Assassins can reach the backline easily
  • Your team cannot protect mid rotations

Advanced Tips

  • Play Odette around the strongest timing of the Mage role.
  • Watch the minimap before forcing fights.
  • Save escape or control skills for enemy dive.
  • Win fight → take turret, Turtle/Lord, or enemy jungle. Do not chase forever.
  • Use Odette when the enemy key escape or control skill is already used.
  • Do not fight randomly before objectives; prepare vision and cooldowns first.
  • After winning a fight, take turret, Turtle, Lord, or enemy jungle instead of chasing too far.
