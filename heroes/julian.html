Player Tip

Useful in-game note

Julian works best when entering from the side. Walking straight into five heroes wastes your pressure and makes you easy to control. After Tough Boots, War Axe you can start playing more confidently. In EXP, side-wave control decides whether your team fights with pressure or loses turret for free. Be careful against kiting, anti-heal. If enemies pick Wanwan, Xavier, avoid ego fights and wait for team setup.

Pro Gameplay Guide

Template
Early Game

Trade around cooldowns and keep lane pressure without dying early.

Mid Game

Join Turtle/Lord fights after managing side wave.

Late Game

Flank or zone enemy backline while protecting your carry if needed.

Combo guide: Gap close → sustain/control skill → main damage → disengage or chase.
Positioning: Side/frontline. Enter from side angles rather than walking straight into CC.

When to Pick

  • Enemy side laner is weak early
  • Your team needs a durable frontliner
  • You can split pressure then join fights
  • You want a Fighter who can play EXP Lane because it can survive lane and join objective fights.

Avoid Picking When

  • Enemy can kite forever
  • Enemy has strong anti-heal
  • You lose lane pressure before first Turtle

Advanced Tips

  • Play Julian around the strongest timing of the Fighter role.
  • Watch the minimap before forcing fights.
  • Save escape or control skills for enemy dive.
  • Win fight → take turret, Turtle/Lord, or enemy jungle. Do not chase forever.
  • Use Julian when the enemy key escape or control skill is already used.
  • Do not fight randomly before objectives; prepare vision and cooldowns first.
  • After winning a fight, take turret, Turtle, Lord, or enemy jungle instead of chasing too far.
