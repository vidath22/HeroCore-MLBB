Player Tip

Useful in-game note

Irithel should not trade HP for no reason in Gold Lane. Farm first, watch enemy roam movement and fight only when you can hit from behind your frontline. After Swift Boots, Corrosion Scythe you can start playing more confidently. In gold lane, dying once before core items can ruin your tempo, so play boring if enemy roam is missing. Be careful against burst assassins, hard crowd control. If enemies pick Zhask, Aamon, avoid ego fights and wait for team setup.

Pro Gameplay Guide

Template
Early Game

Farm safely and avoid unnecessary trades before first core item.

Mid Game

Join fights behind frontline and play around item power spikes.

Late Game

Stay protected, hit the closest safe target, and do not face-check bushes.

Combo guide: Poke/control skill → basic attack chain → save escape/peel for divers.
Positioning: Backline position. Keep tank/roam between you and enemy divers.

When to Pick

  • Your team has a strong frontline
  • Enemy lacks hard dive
  • You can safely reach 2-3 core items
  • You want a Marksman who can play Gold Lane because it needs farm and scales with items.

Avoid Picking When

  • Enemy has multiple dive assassins
  • Your team has no frontline
  • You are forced to first-pick into unknown counters

Advanced Tips

  • Play Irithel around the strongest timing of the Marksman role.
  • Watch the minimap before forcing fights.
  • Save escape or control skills for enemy dive.
  • Win fight → take turret, Turtle/Lord, or enemy jungle. Do not chase forever.
  • Use Irithel when the enemy key escape or control skill is already used.
  • Do not fight randomly before objectives; prepare vision and cooldowns first.
  • After winning a fight, take turret, Turtle, Lord, or enemy jungle instead of chasing too far.
