Player Tip

Useful in-game note

Tigreal should not walk in from the front every time. Hide in fog, let enemies group naturally, then flicker/ult when your damage heroes are close.

Pro Gameplay Guide

Template
Early Game

Give vision, protect lanes, and help secure river/objective control.

Mid Game

Start fights only when damage dealers are close enough to follow.

Late Game

Peel for carries or find clean multi-hero engage near Lord.

Combo guide: Engage skill → crowd control → body block → peel for carry.
Positioning: Frontline and bushes. Control vision before your team walks into river.

When to Pick

  • Your team has strong damage follow-up
  • Enemy carries have no Purify or mobility
  • Objective fights need reliable engage
  • You want a Tank who can play Roam because it helps the team with vision, peel, and setups.

Avoid Picking When

  • Your team refuses to follow engages
  • Enemy has true damage and anti-tank damage
  • You are behind and cannot face-check safely

Advanced Tips

  • Play Tigreal around the strongest timing of the Tank role.
  • Watch the minimap before forcing fights.
  • Save escape or control skills for enemy dive.
  • Win fight → take turret, Turtle/Lord, or enemy jungle. Do not chase forever.
  • Use Tigreal when the enemy key escape or control skill is already used.
  • Do not fight randomly before objectives; prepare vision and cooldowns first.
  • After winning a fight, take turret, Turtle, Lord, or enemy jungle instead of chasing too far.
